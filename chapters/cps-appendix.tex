\label{sec:cps:appendix}

All \cpsslang figures from \fullref[]{chp:cps} are reproduced and completed with
elided parts here, and elided figures are presented here.
These figures contained extensions with definitions required to fully formalize
the compiler correctness results, including booleans with non-dependent
elimination.

      non-bold, sans-serif font}}.
